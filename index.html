It's me again.

When I dreamt of my future career, design and creativity were always at the forefront. I imaged running off to New York City and chasing the faded footsteps of advertising greats down Madison Ave. Instead, I took a detour south and ended up spending 8 years in higher education, first in Trenton, New Jersey and later Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, returning to my roots both physically and professionally. After spending the last five happy years working as a graphic designer, I hope to bring together all of my past experiences and continue to grow and evolve as a designer in the world of user experience.
